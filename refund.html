Refund Policy – Chawp

Effective Date: April 2025 | Version 2.1.3

This policy outlines the circumstances under which refunds may be issued and the procedures for requesting a refund. Please read this policy carefully before placing an order.

When We Might Consider a Refund

Refunds may be issued under the following circumstances:

  • Wrong Items Delivered: When the delivered items do not match the order confirmation and the error is confirmed to be our fault or the vendor's responsibility
  • Non-Delivery: When the order was never delivered and we can confirm the delivery failure through our tracking systems
  • Quality Issues: When the food is completely inedible, spoiled, or contaminated, supported by clear photographic evidence
  • Technical Errors: When duplicate charges occur due to system errors or payment processing issues
  • Restaurant Cancellation: When the restaurant cancels the order after payment has been processed
  • Significant Delay: When the delivery time exceeds the estimated time by more than 60 minutes without prior notification
  • Missing Items: When essential components of the order are missing and cannot be delivered separately
  • Allergy Concerns: When the order contains allergens despite specific instructions to avoid them

When Refunds Aren't Given

Refunds will not be provided in the following cases:

  • Change of Mind: After the order has been placed and confirmed by the restaurant
  • Personal Preferences: If you don't like the taste, temperature, or presentation of the food
  • Delivery Delays: Due to traffic, weather conditions, or other unforeseen circumstances
  • Incomplete Orders: When partial items are missing but can be delivered separately
  • Price Disputes: After the order has been placed and confirmed
  • Promotional Issues: When promotional codes or discounts are not applied correctly
  • Technical Problems: With the app or website that don't affect the actual order
  • Third-Party Issues: Beyond our control, such as payment processor errors

Refund Process

The refund process involves the following steps:

  • Initial Contact: Email chawpgh@gmail.com within 24 hours of the order with your order ID and detailed explanation
  • Documentation: Provide photographic evidence, screenshots, or other relevant documentation
  • Investigation: Our team will review the case within 48 hours
  • Resolution: You will be notified of the decision via email
  • Processing: Approved refunds will be processed within 5-10 business days
  • Confirmation: You will receive a confirmation email once the refund is processed

Refunds will be issued to the original payment method used for the purchase. Processing times may vary depending on your financial institution.

Partial Refunds

In certain cases, partial refunds may be issued:

  • When only a portion of the order is affected
  • When the issue is minor and doesn't warrant a full refund
  • When the customer agrees to a partial resolution
  • When the restaurant offers a partial credit

Special Circumstances

Additional considerations for refund requests:

  • Bulk Orders: Special terms apply for orders above a certain value
  • Subscription Services: Different cancellation and refund policies apply
  • Gift Cards: Non-refundable except where required by law
  • Promotional Items: May have specific refund conditions
