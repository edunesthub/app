Refund Policy – Chawp

Effective Date: April 2025

At Chawp, we aim to deliver food fast, fresh, and without hassle. While we generally do not issue refunds, we understand that sometimes things go wrong.

When We Might Consider a Refund

We may issue a full or partial refund if:

  • The wrong items were delivered, and it’s confirmed to be our fault or the vendor’s.
  • Your order was never delivered, and we can confirm the failure.
  • The food was completely inedible, and there’s clear, honest evidence (e.g., photos).

When Refunds Aren’t Given

Refunds will not be provided in cases such as:

  • You changed your mind after ordering.
  • You didn’t like the taste or temperature of the food.
  • The delivery was delayed due to traffic, weather, or other unforeseen issues.

How to Request a Refund

Send an email to support@chawp.app within 24 hours of your order. Include your order ID and a brief explanation. We’ll review your situation fairly and respond quickly. If your case is legit, we’ve got you.

We don’t play games—but we also don’t like being played.
