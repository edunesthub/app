Privacy Policy – Chawp

Effective Date: April 2025 | Version 2.1.3

This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information. Please read this policy carefully before using our services.

Information Collection

We collect various types of information to provide and improve our services:

  • Personal Information: Full name, email, phone number, delivery address
  • Order Data: Order history, preferences, dietary restrictions
  • Device Information: IP address, device type, operating system
  • Location Data: GPS coordinates for delivery tracking
  • Usage Data: App interactions, feature usage patterns
  • Payment Data: Payment method details, transaction history

How We Use Your Data

Your information helps us provide better service:

  • Order Processing: Fulfilling your food delivery requests
  • Customer Support: Assisting with inquiries and issues
  • Service Improvement: Enhancing app features and performance
  • Security: Protecting against fraud and unauthorized access
  • Communication: Sending important updates and offers

Data Protection

We implement strong security measures:

  • Encryption: All sensitive data is encrypted
  • Access Control: Strict staff data access policies
  • Monitoring: 24/7 security system monitoring
  • Compliance: Following data protection laws

While we implement strong security measures, no system is 100% secure. We'll notify you promptly of any security incidents.

Your Rights

You have control over your data:

  • Access: View your personal data
  • Correction: Update incorrect information
  • Deletion: Request data removal
  • Export: Download your data
