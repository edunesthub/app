Privacy Policy – Chawp

Effective Date: April 2025

At Chawp, your privacy is a priority. We only collect what we need to deliver your food safely and efficiently—and nothing more.

What We Collect

  • Name, email, phone number, delivery address
  • Order history and payment info (securely processed)
  • Device and browser data (to improve performance)

How We Use It

  • To deliver your food and send order updates
  • To improve your app experience
  • To troubleshoot issues and support your requests

What We Don’t Do

  • Sell your data to anyone—ever
  • Use your personal info for ads
  • Store sensitive payment info (we use secure third-party processors)

Your Rights

  • You can request to see, update, or delete your data anytime
  • You can opt out of certain communications (except critical updates)

Security

We use strong encryption and best practices to protect your data. No shady stuff.

Questions?

Email us at support@chawp.app and we’ll get back to you fast.
